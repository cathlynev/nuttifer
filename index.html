About Me

Nuttifer is a modern health brand founded in 2025, offering a unique twist on traditional Indonesian tempeh by using almonds instead of soybeans. Through a careful fermentation process, Nuttifer creates nutritious, plant-based products that blend heritage with innovation. Focused on wellness and sustainability, Nuttifer brings the richness of tempeh to a new generation in a healthy, delicious way.
